Linear algebra is essential to many optimization techniques in addition to statistics (especially forecasting). In many, if not all industries, data will be formatted in vectors and/or matrices. Even a set of inputs can be paired with outputs as vectors and/or matrices. For those who worked with spreadsheets before, you already have exposure to vectors and matrices in the form of tables. Typically, the numbers in those tables will involve constraints, KPIs, metrics of a variable (coefficients), etc. This is designed to be a short introduction to linear algebra towards developing working knowledge. These topics include:

  • Systems of Linear Equations
  • Linear Independence and Dependence
  • Determinants
  • Orthogonality
  • Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors
